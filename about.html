Welcome to Au Knowledge

My name is Matthew Austin and I am the founder of Au Knowledge, a Knowledge Management firm located in Buffalo, NY.

What is Knowledge Management you may ask? It's simply the study of how to use knowledge to solve problems. Knowledge Managers are much like Architects. Just as an Architect uses materials such as wood, steel and brick to design buildings, a Knowledge Manager uses data, information and knowledge to design solutions.

Organizations that effectively discover, create and deploy knowledge will thrive in the digital age. For that reason I founded Au Knowledge to help others adopt their own Knowledge Management practice.
